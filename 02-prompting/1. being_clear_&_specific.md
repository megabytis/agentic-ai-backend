# Effective AI Prompting Principles

## **1. Be Clear**

### Core Principles:

- Use simple, straightforward language
- State your requirements explicitly
- Begin prompts with a clear task statement

### Bad Example ❌:

"I need to know about those things people put on their roofs that use sun – those solar panel things, I think they’re called"

### Good Example ✅:

"Write three paragraphs about how solar panels work"

---

## **2. Be Direct**

### Core Principles:

- Use **instructions**, not questions
- Employ direct action verbs
- Be specific about desired output format

### Bad Example ❌:

"I was reading about renewable energy and geothermal energy sounds neat. What countries use it?"

### Good Example ✅:

"Identify three countries that use geothermal energy. Include generation statistics for each."

---

## **Key Action Verbs to Use:**

- Write
- Create
- Generate
- Identify
- Explain
- Compare
- Analyze

## **Prompt Structure Template:**

[Action verb] [specific output] about/of [topic].
[Include/Exclude specific details].
[Specify format/length if needed].

## **Key Takeaways:**

1. **Lead with the task** - State what you want first
2. **Use command language** - Tell, don't ask
3. **Specify output format** - Be explicit about what you expect
4. **Keep it simple** - Avoid vague or conversational language
